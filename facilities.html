Our School Facilities

Welcome to Vivekananda High School, where we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and empowering learning environment for our students. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our top-notch facilities designed to enhance every aspect of the educational journey.

Transportation Excellence

Ensuring the safety and convenience of our students, we provide a reliable bus transportation service. Our modern fleet is equipped with the latest amenities, guaranteeing a secure and comfortable journey to and from school.

Innovative Learning Spaces

Embark on a journey of interactive learning in our cutting-edge smart classrooms. Fitted with advanced technology, these classrooms create a dynamic and engaging atmosphere, fostering a love for learning among our students.

Gateway to Knowledge

Immerse yourself in a world of knowledge at our well-stocked library. With a diverse collection of books and resources, our library provides students with the tools they need to excel in their academic pursuits.

Tech-Forward Education

Cultivate digital literacy and technical skills in our modern computer labs. These labs boast the latest technology, offering students hands-on experience with various software and applications.

Scientific Exploration

Ignite curiosity through hands-on experiments in our specialized science laboratories. Our labs provide students with the opportunity to explore and understand scientific principles in a practical and engaging manner.

Active Living

Promoting a healthy lifestyle, our school offers a range of sports facilities. From expansive playing fields to indoor courts, students have the chance to actively participate in sports and physical activities, fostering holistic development.
