About Vivekananda High School

Vivekananda High School was inaugurated in 1983 through the hard work of Ms.Kamalesh Kumari. The school is named after Swami Vivekananda, emphasizing the reintegration of Indian culture by synthesizing ancient but ever-relevant spiritual values adapted to the needs of modern times.

Our school is dedicated to providing a transformative learning experience. We believe in a well-rounded education that focuses on the development of critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. We measure success by the value we add to each individual student.

We aim to uphold high standards of ethical, moral, and civic conduct, fostering tolerance, cooperation, and teamwork. Our goal is to produce leaders committed to lifelong learning while nurturing the future with love and care, maintaining a balance between modernity and tradition.

Our Mission and Values

Our mission is to foster holistic development and provide unparalleled education that transcends traditional boundaries. We are committed to cultivating a nurturing and inclusive environment that champions academic achievement, character development, and a lifelong passion for learning.
